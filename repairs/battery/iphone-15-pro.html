iPhone 15 Pro Battery Replacement Tutorial

This battery replacement tutorial slows the job down for first-time repairers: open the display carefully, disconnect power early, track every bracket screw, release adhesive safely, and test the phone before closing it.

iPhone 15 Pro battery replacement for first-time repairers

What this tutorial does

Use this page when you want to replace a worn battery that has poor runtime, unexpected shutdowns, or swelling risk. Read one step, compare the photo to your phone, complete that action, then move to the next step.

Main risk

A lithium-ion battery can be dangerous if punctured, bent hard, overheated, or pried with a sharp metal tool.

Do before opening

  • Power the iPhone off and clear a bright workspace.
  • Keep a screw mat or labeled paper map beside the phone.
  • Read the screw table before removing brackets with mixed lengths.

Before closing

Before closing the phone, confirm the battery connector is seated, no adhesive liner or loose screw remains inside, and the device boots from the new battery.

New repairer rule: never force a connector, battery adhesive, display edge, or glass fragment just because the next photo looks simple. Stop, re-check the model-specific photo, and keep every screw in its original location.
