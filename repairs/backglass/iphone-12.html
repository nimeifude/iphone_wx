iPhone 12 back glass replacement for first-time repairers

What this tutorial does

Use this page when you want to replace damaged rear glass while protecting cameras, wireless charging parts, cables, and the battery. Read one step, compare the photo to your phone, complete that action, then move to the next step.

Main risk

Rear glass work can involve heat, sharp broken glass, camera surrounds, wireless charging hardware, and model-specific internal parts.

Do before opening

  • Power the iPhone off and clear a bright workspace.
  • Keep a screw mat or labeled paper map beside the phone.
  • Read the screw table before removing brackets with mixed lengths.

Before closing

Before final sealing, inspect camera openings, wireless charging parts, flex cables, glass fragments, and every screw location shown in the guide.

New repairer rule: never force a connector, battery adhesive, display edge, or glass fragment just because the next photo looks simple. Stop, re-check the model-specific photo, and keep every screw in its original location.

Step By Step

Back Glass Replacement photos and instructions

These model-specific repair steps are arranged in photo order. The photo shows where you are working; the instruction text explains the action and measurements supplied by the source guide.

01

Laser Marking

Beginner check: Clear loose glass and watch the camera and wireless charging area before moving to the next step.

Put the back glass of iPhone 12 under the focus positioning probe of the laser marking machine. After positioning, mark the back glass area except for the camera and wireless charging area. We haven't marked the wireless charging area to avoid damage.

Screw and part rule: keep parts in removal order. If this step removes a bracket or screw, its original position matters during reassembly.

02

Back Glass Separation

Beginner check: Work from the bracket or connector edge shown in the photo. Do not pry under nearby board parts.

A new member, Glass Breaking Pen, is coming to our back glass refurbishing tools family. Break the back glass with Glass Breaking Pen and remove the shards with Pry Bar. When breaking the wireless charging area, you can see that the magnets are glued to the glass. Hence the back glass fix solution of iPhone 11 is not suitable for iPhone 12. If we stick to our old method, all magnets will fall off and become separate. The subsequent recovering process will be harder. 03

Back Glass Separation

Beginner check: Clear loose glass and watch the camera and wireless charging area before moving to the next step.

We should remove the wireless charging coil and then mark. The magnet array and shield can be easily removed after marking.

Screw and part rule: keep parts in removal order. If this step removes a bracket or screw, its original position matters during reassembly.

04

Back Glass Separation

Beginner check: Use patience here. Softened adhesive and shallow tools are safer than forcing the panel open.

Then we remove the glass around the camera. Please do not touch the camera lens. After the glass is completely removed, mark the back again to remove residual adhesive.

05

Back Glass Gluing

Beginner check: Use patience here. Softened adhesive and shallow tools are safer than forcing the panel open.

Clamp a new back glass with enlarged camera holes down to the middle frame first to see if it fits. Apply some adhesive evenly on the middle frame. Put the back glass in position and press it gently against the middle frame.

06

Back Glass Gluing

Beginner check: Use patience here. Softened adhesive and shallow tools are safer than forcing the panel open.

Press the back glass further against the middle frame with Press Clamp. The adhesive has set after 10 minutes. Take the back cover out. Check if the gap is too large, if there is adhesive overflow, and if the fit is smooth.

Screw and part rule: keep parts in removal order. If this step removes a bracket or screw, its original position matters during reassembly.

07

Back Glass Gluing

Beginner check: Use patience here. Softened adhesive and shallow tools are safer than forcing the panel open.

After the back glass gluing is finished, we install the magnet array and shield. Apply some B-7000 Adhesive on the magnet circle to adhere the magnet array to the back. Please make sure that the opening of the magnet array is placed in the right position as in the picture. Stick the magnet that we accidentally cut out back on and glue the alignment magnet. Last but not least, assemble the phone and test the MagSafe function. The MagSafe function is normal.
