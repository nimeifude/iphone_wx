Repair Safety

Before you open an iPhone

  • Discharge the battery below 25% before any repair involving the battery or internal connectors.
  • Keep every screw mapped to its original location. A long screw in the wrong hole can damage the logic board.
  • Back glass repair is more difficult than battery or screen repair and may involve heat, glass shards, wireless charging coils, and camera hardware.
  • Always verify the exact model before following a guide. Regional variants may have different SIM, antenna, or internal layouts.

Model coverage is based on Apple’s official iPhone model identification page. Repair pages cite iFixit or another traceable source when real teardown photos and screw measurements are available.
